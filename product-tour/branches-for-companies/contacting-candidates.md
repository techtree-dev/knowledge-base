### How to search candidates:

1. Login & access your ‘Candidate’ dashboard
2. Click the 'Prospects' tab
3. Search the candidate pool with the following filters:
   * Current Location
   * Current Highlights
   * Experience
   * Roles
   * Tags
4. Review candidates' LinkedIn profile & CVs&#x20;
